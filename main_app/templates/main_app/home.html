Empowering California's Youth

Educate California's mission is to help families, schools and nonprofit organizations prepare youth for life after high school.

Through our in-person and virtual LifePrep Academy training sessions, we provide students and their parents with essential knowledge, skills and resources that students need to graduate - college and workforce ready. Our resources and workshops are vital to the community because they address California’s more than 600:1 student-to-counselor ratio.

By empowering families to advocate for their children and to partner with their children’s school counselors, we will help more low and moderate-income youth become the educated and skilled workers California communities need for economic success. Since 1998, we have helped thousands of students plan their future and achieve their goals.
