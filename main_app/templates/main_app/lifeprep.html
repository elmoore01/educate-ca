LifePrep Academy

ENGAGING PARENTS, EMPOWERING YOUTH

The LifePrep Academy program supports family engagement and our future workforce by preparing middle and high school students for life after high school. We provide students and families with essential tools and resources through our in-person and virtual LifePrep Academy training sessions.

Through our LifePrep Academy program, we teach students and their parents the college knowledge, attitudes and skills they need to graduate from high school college-ready. LifePrep Academy workshop participants learn academic planning strategies, college and career options, how to access internet-based resources, the various types of financial aid available to them, and much more.
